WELCOME TO ONTARIO COMMUTER CONNECT!

Commuter Connect is a trip planning and commuter-matching service that provides a one-stop-shop trip planning tool for improving your commute, whether you carpool, vanpool, walk, bike, or take transit. Through Commuter Connect's easy-to-use interface and mobile app, you can search for all available transportation options for your commute by simply entering an origin and destination.



Reduce Ontario's carbon footprint by choosing an eco-friendly commute. Whether you want to help the environment, save money, or reduce stress, Commuter Connect helps you to find a better way to commute. Start logging your commutes and you might win a prize. Every mile makes a difference!
